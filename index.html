The Italian Resturant

This is the beginning.

It is the middle of July, shining blue skies with clouds painted across the horizon. The sun is starting to decline, casting a golden hue through the trees. You are in the city with your family on vacation. It is nearing dinner time, and you are walking to the cities best Italian resturant, with thousands of five star reviews. Or it should be. You have a reservation set for 7pm, but you pass by a clothing store that catches your eye. It is 6:45, and the resturant is still a few blocks away. Do you

  • Go in the store
or
  • go to the resturant?
